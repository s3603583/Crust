About Us!

Our team proposes to design a game for Mobile devices, PC and MAC OS. We as a team want to create a rich and complex game which will be free to play for everyone. This game will be a side scroller available for PC and Mac OSX, it will be coded in unity with c# with proper tasks delegated for each team member. This game will be free to play and it will allow temporary entertainment for the user.

Motivation

  • The game will be a side scroller. Our character is a pizza in an australian restaurant escaping being topped with pineapples by Anthony Clapp. Then, the pizza escapes the restaurant and starts going through various Australian sceneries. Ranging from the restaurant, city, highway, bush, desert and an abandoned airstrip in the desert. As the background scenery changes, so will the obstacles (kangaroos, cars, cactuses) pineapples will be the only constant.

    Power ups may be included, like peppers and olives. The goal is to get to italy to spend leisure time with pepperonis and mozzarellas. We can probably make the game keep running indefinitely after that.


  • Our motivation came from our team common interest in gaming. Because of that, the idea of creating a game was the perfect match for us. Currently with gaming industry sheer size, many softwares are available that allow people to create games from scratch quite easily. Side scroller games provide enjoyment with its simplicity. We will be targeting audiences of all ages, but mostly teens. This project will allow possible future employers to see that with a team, we are able to provide a close to finished product and a proper plan. They will be able to see that we are able to work as a group and accomplish tasks even as first year students.
